Neon Protocol // Manual

Welcome, Broker. Your objective is to fulfill corporate data contracts and reach the Target Score before your connection severs (Uploads run out).

🎴 The Data (Cards)

You hold 6 data chips. Each has a Value (1-9) and a Color Segment. Click chips to select or deselect them.

📋 Executing Contracts

Read the 3 active contracts. Select the exact cards that match the description, then click Execute Upload to submit them and score points. Submitting burns 1 Upload turn.

💸 The Economy

If your hand is useless, you can reroll selected cards—but it costs 200 Points. Spend your score wisely, or you will fail the level.

⚙️ Root Upgrades

Beating a level grants you a permanent upgrade. Draft synergies like Redline Injection or Data Compression to build massive point multipliers.

Sentinel Operations Codex

Welcome to the Void. You are humanity's last Sentinel. Your mission is to survive infinite sectors by managing your Action Points (AP) and eliminating the Alien threat.

⚡ Action Points (AP)

Every move and attack costs AP. You start with 3 AP per turn. If you run out, you must END TURN, allowing the enemies to advance.

🎯 Combat Tactics

  • SHOOT (1 AP): Fires in a straight line. Deals 3 Dmg. Blocked by walls.
  • GRENADE (2 AP): Hits a 3x3 area. Deals 3 Dmg. Ignores walls. Will hurt you if you are in the blast radius!

👾 Hostiles

Aliens move 1 tile per turn. If they end their turn next to you, you lose 1 HP. Every 5 Sectors, a BOSS spawns. Bosses have massive health and hit for 3 HP.

🧬 Upgrades & Permutations

Defeating a Boss allows you to draft a permanent upgrade. Combine perks like Vampire Rounds with Heavy Shrapnel to build a devastating tactical engine.
